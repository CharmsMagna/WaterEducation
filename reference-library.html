Reference Library

UK Government & Regulatory Bodies

  • Natural England - Nutrient Neutrality Guidance
    Official guidance on nutrient neutrality, mitigation strategies, and planning requirements.
  • Environment Agency - Water Quality & Nutrient Pollution
    Covers regulations on nutrient pollution, river basin management plans, and permitting.
  • DEFRA (Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs)
    Policies on sustainable water management, agriculture, and environmental protection.

Water & Nutrient Management Strategies

  • UK Water Industry Research (UKWIR)
    Research reports on nutrient neutrality, water reuse, and efficiency.
  • CIRIA - Sustainable Drainage Systems (SuDS)
    Best practices for integrating SuDS, wetlands, and other mitigation measures.
  • Waterwise - Water Efficiency & Demand Reduction
    Strategies to reduce water use and mitigate nutrient loads.

Scientific & Research Institutions

  • UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology (UKCEH)
    Research on nutrient loading, land-use impact, and catchment-based approaches.
  • James Hutton Institute - Agriculture & Water Pollution
    Studies on phosphorus, nitrogen, and farm-level nutrient management.
  • British Geological Survey (BGS) - Groundwater & Nutrient Transport
    Resources on groundwater contamination and nutrient pathways.

International & European References

  • European Environment Agency (EEA)
    EU policies on nutrient pollution, catchment management, and sustainability.
  • United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP)
    Global initiatives on water quality, nutrient reduction, and SDGs.
  • International Water Association (IWA)
    Research on wastewater treatment, stormwater reuse, and nutrient capture.

Practical Tools & Nutrient Calculators

  • Catchment Based Approach (CaBA)
    Supports stakeholders with nutrient offsetting and mitigation strategies.
  • Nutrient Accounting Tool (NE)
    Helps developers and planners calculate nutrient loads in new developments.
